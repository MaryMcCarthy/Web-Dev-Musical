Phantom of the Opera, The Musical

Deformed since birth, a bitter man known as the Phantom lives in the sewers underneath the Opera House in Paris. He falls in love with the unknown chorus singer Christine, and privately tutors her while scaring the rest of the opera house and demanding Christine be given lead roles. This musical is a tear jerker and is filled with brilliant actors and actresses who expertly convey this emotion through their harrowing songs and brilliant acting.
