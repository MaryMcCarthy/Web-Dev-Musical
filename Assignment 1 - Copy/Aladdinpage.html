Aladdin, The Musical

In the town of Agrabah, Princess Jasmine is feeling trapped by her father’s insisent need to find her a royal groom. Meanwhile, the Sultan’s right-hand, Jafar, is plotting to over throw him. When Jasmine sneaks out of the palace, she forms an instant connection with Aladdin, a charming and reformed thief. After being discovered together, Aladdin is sentenced to death, but Jafar saves him by ordering him to fetch a lamp from the Cave of Wonders. in this lamp, there’s a Genie, and once Aladdin unwittingly lets this one out, anything will happen! stealing it right from under Jafar's nose. This delightful musical is filled with twists and turns, and charming songs and characters that make the show even more enjoyable.
